Tours Around Bozeman

We offer exciting and adventurous bike tours around the Bozeman area. Our tours are run by local experienced guides.

Emerald Lake

Trail Location

South of Bozeman down Hyalite Canyon road, about a mile past the waterfall.

Trail Type

100% single track mountain bike trail.

Distance

9 miles out and back

Skill Level

Advanced skills

Cost

$35 per person without bike. $85 per person incluing bike rental.

Sourdough Ridge Loop

Trail Location

Located on the east edge of Bozeman's city limits.

Trail Type

1.6 miles on paved road; 1.9 miles on singletrack or gravel path.

Distance

3.5 total miles

Skill Level

Mostly easy, with two short but strenuous climbs.

Cost

$25 per person without bike. $75 per person including bike rental.
